Services

What we do

Every service is built around one goal: more of the right people finding your business in the places they're already looking.

SEO Audit & Optimization
A thorough look at your website's technical health, on-page signals, and local keyword targeting. You get a clear picture of what's working and what to fix, without the jargon.
GBP Optimization & Audit
Your GBP is often the first thing a customer sees. Every field, category, photo, and post gets dialed in so you show up higher in local results and actually look worth calling.
Website Creation
Fast, clean websites built to turn visitors into customers. Not a template dropped into a builder. Something that actually fits your business, your tone, and the people you want to reach.
Citation Building
Your business name, address, and phone number listed accurately and consistently across Yelp, Apple Maps, Bing, and dozens of other directories. It sounds simple, but it is one of the most important trust signals Google looks for.
AI Search & More
Search is changing. More people are getting answers directly from AI tools like ChatGPT and Google's AI Overview before they ever click a result. Near stays current with how these systems work and helps position your business as a trusted source across both traditional search and AI-powered results. Plus local content, review guidance, and anything else that keeps you in front of the right people.
